About Me

Hi there! My name is Madison and you're looking at my first attempt at coding! This is my portfolio showcasing my lovely works of art that don't include latte art (okay, maybe I'll throw a little coffee on here, too).


I was born in Colorado and have lived all over, traveled to 12 countries, and I'm looking for a career that can keep up with my crazy ambitions and goals. I've been in the coffee industry for 8 years now and I'm ready to get out from behind the espresso machine, so to speak.


If you want to follow along on my adventures, I can promise lots of pictures of travels and coffee, but most importantly, tons of pictures of my 100lb dog, Oakley. Cheers!
